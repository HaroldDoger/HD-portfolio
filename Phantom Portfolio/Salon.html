Sandi's Salon

Sandi's Salon is a beautifully designed and easy-to-use website that offers an exceptional experience for customers looking for their next glow up.

Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this website is equipped with a gallery showcase that displays the salon's latest hairstyles and services, giving customers a clear understanding of the quality of work they provide. The website features an appointment booking system that allows customers to easily book appointments at their convenience and a services section that contains all the treatments and plans that Sandi's Salon has to offer.

Sandi's Salon is fully responsive and since it's implementation has vastly increased costumer acquisition and revenue for the business.
