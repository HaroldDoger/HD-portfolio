League of Legends Skins True or False

League of Legends True or False is a dynamic and interactive full stack web application centered around the massively popular MOBA game, League of Legends; Implemented with JavaScript, React, Node.js, MongoDB, Express, and HTML/CSS.

League true or false is a unique and engaging experience for players of all skill levels. At its core, the app leverages the RIOT GAMES API to generate both real and fake skin names for the game's champions. Players are then challenged to determine the authenticity of these names, with correct answers contributing to their overall high score and ranking on the leaderboard. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a casual fan, League true or false offers an exciting and challenging way to enhance your knowledge and trivia within the world of League of Legends.
