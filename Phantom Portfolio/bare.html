Bare Bot

Barebot is a fully capable audio player bot for Discord, designed to bring music and audio to your server. With a multitude of functions and commands, Barebot is the perfect addition to your server.

Some of its key features include:

Bass boosting audio effect: Change the bass level of the audio with a single command.

Queue management: View, shuffle, bump, move, loop and clear the server queue.

Song grab and lyrics: Save the current song to your Direct Messages and see its lyrics.

Playback controls: Pause, resume, skip, and seek through the music.

Volume control: Adjust the volume to your preference.

Help and stats: Get information about the bot and its commands.

Barebot is available in two versions, built with either Python and Flask or JavaScript and Node. The bot was built with replit, a cloud-based coding platform, making it accessible from anywhere, at any time.

If you're looking for a powerful audio player for your Discord server, Barebot is the perfect solution!
