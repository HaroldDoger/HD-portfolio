Amicus Marketing

Amicus Marketing is a dynamic and user-friendly site for clients to contact and find their next full-service marketing agency.

The site was built with the user in mind, utilizing responsive design to ensure an optimal viewing experience on any device, from desktop computers to mobile phones. I utilized HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to bring the design to life, creating a modern and visually appealing site that showcases Amicus Marketing's services, team, and results.

The site is intuitive and easy to navigate, making it simple for visitors to find the information they need and get in touch with Amicus Marketing. The site also features beautiful imagery and a clean, professional design that accurately reflects the level of expertise and professionalism offered by the agency.
